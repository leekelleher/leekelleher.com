Twitter

Hello! Thanks for following the link from my Twitter profile. I guess you'd like to know a little more about me and my Twitter account?

In recent years I have built up a reputation as an Umbraco specialist. I am involved with the Umbraco project at many levels, from organising local events/meet-ups, developing packages and contributing to the core product. So the majority of my tweets will be on Umbraco related subjects.

If you aren't interested in Umbraco, ASP.NET, C#, or general web-development, then please do feel free to unfollow me. As for who I follow on Twitter, this depends on my mood and current interests – if I don't follow you back, it is nothing personal.

Of course, I'm human too – so if you are family, friend, or we've meet in "real life" … then you are welcome to add me as a friend on Facebook; where I mostly post cute photos of my kids and I'll never mention programming!

If you are looking to get in contact with me for my professional Umbraco services, please get do get in touch with me via my company, Umbrella.

For any feedback, comments or questions, feel free to ask me via my contact page, on Twitter, or on GitHub.
